Evidence contract

Access is a set of indicator lanes, not a verdict

Each lane keeps its own geography role, unit, period, source, and caveat. Unlike measures are never blended into a “who is winning” score.

01

03

Read before interpreting

What this layer can support

  • It can show U.S.-reported imports from China and other selected origins.
  • It can show U.S. domestic exports to China and other selected destinations.
  • It cannot show the PRC's total imports from the world.
  • It cannot identify mine origin, processing control, ownership, route, end use, or strategic importance.
  • HTS4 categories are product baskets; broad or mixed baskets are labeled in the selector.

Statistical method

Rules that prevent an attractive chart from becoming a false claim

Read the full methodology →

Period integrity

The default view compares January–April in every year. The alternate view mixes full years through 2025 with January–April 2026 and labels the break.

Geography integrity

China and Hong Kong remain separate. Origin, destination, mine location, processing location, and ownership are never treated as synonyms.

Product integrity

HTS4 categories are shown as traded-product categories. Mixed baskets such as ferroalloys or minor metals are not silently assigned to one mineral.

Measurement integrity

Customs value and F.A.S. value retain their source bases. Physical quantities are not added across unlike units.

Gap integrity

Absent source rows, source blanks, suppression flags, and reported zeros are distinct. V3 does not interpolate or impute.

Derivation integrity

Any future calculated share or concentration measure must expose its formula, inputs, coverage, and missing-value rule.
